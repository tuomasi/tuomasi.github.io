Bivouac is a project management tool much like Trello. Throughout my MSc in St Andrews, this was the first project which had us do something really useful/cool (granted, us freshlings couldn't do much in the first semester since we were busy learning the basics of it all).

The project makes use of the Google Drive API and the Google Drive Realtime API, primarily to store projects and allow collaboration.
